Mendoza, the Andes & Uco Valley


Where style, flavor and adventure form the richest blend

Mendoza and the Uco Valley are regional leaders in the type of tourism we respect: diverse, sustainable and open to all tastes and styles. Straddling the Andes Mountains’ highest peaks, Mendoza is a gateway to wine and fine dining, adventure and relaxation - and arguably one of Argentina’s most beautiful cities. For its part, nearby Uco Valley is home to some of the world’s most prestigious and technologically advanced vineyards where visits are integral: wine tasting, hands-on production lessons, outdoor activities and a range of accommodation options.

The region is a perfect hub for adventure activities like hiking, rafting or multi-day criollo horseback rides through the Andes. But undoubtedly, the focus is on wine. With wines of truly unique terroir nourished by the region’s soils and glacier water, Mendoza and the Uco Valley offer any number of vineyard experiences — from the avant-garde and experimental through to the traditional.
Emerging from the region near Mendoza capital and spreading into the diverse Uco Valley wine producing region, you’ll taste astonishing wines (and even olive oil), learn about production techniques, enjoy picnics, bike rides, and more.


CRAFTED IDEAS FOR YOU

▸ Wine tasting at modern avant-garde Mendoza vineyards or centuries-old estates

▸ Visit the experimental Valle de Uco vineyards with their endless sub-varieties

▸ Discover the fine crafts of the region’s traditional wool sewers

▸ Aconcagua: Visit the bases surrounding the Andes’s highest peak with day treks offering breathtaking views

▸ Special vineyard experiences including picnics, bike rides, cooking classes

▸ Enjoy harvest season activities at the region’s vineyards and the unique experience of wine and snow in winter
