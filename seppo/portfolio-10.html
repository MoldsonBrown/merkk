Santiago de Chile & Central Valley


A thriving cosmopolitan capital tucked between mountain and sea

This prosperous and ambitious city blends loud modernity and unassuming tradition in arguably one of South America’s most distinct capitals. Tucked between mountain and sea, Santiago is as much its bohemian neighborhoods and centuries-old downtown as it is Sanhattan, the tongue-in-cheek name given to the city’s more affluent pockets. The capital is also your convenient gateway to the region’s many natural pleasures, with breathtaking coastal cities Valparaíso and Viña del Mar nearby, Easter Island just a plane flight away, plus day trips to exquisite vineyards and the Andes Mountains.

Retrace the life and times of famed Chilean Nobel-prize winning author Pablo Neruda at any one of his three out-of-this-world homesteads. The capital is also oh so convenient, with a geographical location to die for — the Andes mountains and ski resorts a little over an hour to the east, the country’s enchanting artistic capital Valparaíso and beach city Viña to the west. Wine lovers in particular will enjoy Santiago and the Central Valley, a region awash with world-class vineyards, with Mendoza just a mountain away in Argentina.


CRAFTED IDEAS FOR YOU

In Santiago:

▸ Stroll through Mercado Central

▸ Climb or take a gondola to Cerro San Cristobal

▸ Enjoy the easy nightlife in Barrio Bellavista or get a taste of bohemia in Barrio Italia

▸ Appreciate Chile’s long, complex political history from Plaza de Armas

▸ Start the Pablo Neruda trail at La Chascona

▸ Get lost on Chile’s expansive, modern underground network

Surroundings:

▸ Taste the exceptional quality of Chilean wine at vineyards in the Maipo, Colchagua or Casablanca valleys

▸ Explore the fascinating port city and Chilean cultural capital of Valparaíso, or Valpo for short

▸ Enjoy high-end beach life at Viña del Mar (but be prepared for the cold waters of the Pacific ocean)

▸ Discover more Pablo Neruda at his former Isla Negra (Viña) and La Sebastiana (Valparaíso) homes

▸ Zig-zag your way through the Andes’ most breathtaking pass to beautiful Mendoza

▸ Reach the heart of the Pacific at Chile’s Easter Island
