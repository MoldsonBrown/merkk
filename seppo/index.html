Who we are

Craft & Travel is a boutique travel agency based in Buenos Aires.
Our team is led by Alina and Ana, a pair of intrepid travellers born in Argentina to migrant families where food, craft and tradition rule.
Founded in 2019, our offerings are an answer to the creative yearnings that bring thousands of people each year to Argentina and Chile in search of the places, people, sounds and flavors forged by our countries’ unique isolation and rich histories.
