Craft & Travel is a boutique travel agency based in Buenos Aires.
Our team is led by Alina and Ana, a pair of intrepid travellers born in Argentina to migrant families where food, craft and tradition rule.
Founded in 2019, our offerings are an answer to the creative yearnings that bring thousands of people each year to Argentina and Chile in search of the places, people, sounds and flavors forged by our countries’ unique isolation and rich histories.



Across the region, our work is rooted in craft, creativity and our own personal passions as experienced travellers ourselves. From our signature journeys through to homespun trajectories, we believe in the idea of travel with purpose. We go well beyond surface-level sightseeing to offer a more affectionate engagement with our local cultures, societies and landscapes. Covering all the major territories in Argentina and Chile, our journeys are the product of our expertise as well as a conviction of the need to enrich the lives of everyone involved in the tourism chain: locals, partners and travellers alike. We work conscientiously with open ears and big hearts, and a commitment to the transformation and betterment of our industry.

Travel agents Ana and Alina bring over 15 years’ experience in inbound tourism and hospitality in Argentina and Chile. Having shared many a trip to the heart of both countries, as former workmates and friends, they boast a deep and synchronized understanding of the idiosyncrasies and complexities that define our region. Born to migrant families, their approach to tourism in Argentina and Chile is defined by curiosity and hard work, with their own personal passions interlaced into all the packages and tours we offer.


PARTNERSHIPS

What we do & How we think

Connecting partners and special interest groups with Argentina & Chile

Travelling is, in most cases, the fulfillment of a dream. At Craft & Travel, we encourage curiosity and personal fulfilment in all our tour offerings. Whether you’re a tour operator looking for local connections, or a member of a special interest group wanting to organize a highly specific encounter with local connoisseurs or experts, we’ll help you to organize a program finely balanced between traditional journeys and the unique forms of engagement happening in today’s tourism.

Our working relationships are based on a particular set of conditions: nurturing the integrity of our region’s traditions and its natural environments, while developing tours and packages with the attention and sensitivity that our partners and clients seek in us as experienced local operators.

Take a look at the full schedule of Destinations in Argentina and Chile or contact us to discuss your ideas.

How it all began

Alina and I were sitting at a coffee shop in Copenhagen. We realized that everything we’d done so far on our trip to Europe was rooted in our passions and hobbies. I had done a painting course in Rome while Alina had learned to make dough in France. This was the way forward for us in the travel industry and we could now create a space in Argentina and Chile for travellers to expand and enrich the activities that fulfil their daily lives back home."

- Ana

Our journeys

Where to go in Argentina and Chile?

With its pulsating capitals and highly intriguing provincial communities, Argentina and Chile are home to a rare expression of cultural and geographical diversity arguably second-to-none in the Americas. Ancient traditions intertwined with the remnants of colonialism in the north. The blissful solitude of being engulfed in unique monumental forms of nature in the south. And everything in between.

At Craft & Travel, we know the people you’ll meet and the places you’ll stay. We love this region of the world... our own! We offer specially crafted tours in Argentina and Chile that take away the effort in planning and make coming all this way an easy decision. Dream, fantasize and create with our expert local agents!
